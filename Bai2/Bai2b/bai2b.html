How to make Chocolate Cake (★★★★)


A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler or microwave
  2. Whisk the eggs and sugar until the mixture is fluffy and soft.
  3. Mix the melted chocolate into the egg mixture.
  4. Sift the flour and salt into the mixture.
